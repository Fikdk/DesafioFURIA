    FURIA is a Brazilian esports organization.

    History

    On December 20, 2019, FURIA Esports acquired the Uppercut esports organization to form FURIA Uppercut.[1][2]. Later in the same day it was also announced that the team would rename to FURIA Esports after the conclusion of CBLOL 2020 Split 1.[3]. Since 2024, the team has been known only as FURIA.

    Trivia

    • Reached the top 1 spot in the ranking of teams with the most LP in the world with 7837 LP in April 27th, 2025, according to DPMLOL, surpassing even G2.[4]
